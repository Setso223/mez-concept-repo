Golf Course

The Duke’s championship golf course gives magnificent panoramic views over the town of St Andrews and stunning coastline. Play is breathtaking – in every sense.The 18 hole course is set up as two loops of 9 holes – enabling us to change the course’s layout to suit multiple formats and competitions. Five separate tee positions on each hole offers both flexibility in overall course length and difficulty to appeal to golfers at every level. The Duke’s offers a range of first class facilities including a fully stocked pro shop, driving range, short game practice facilities and a fleet of buggies.
